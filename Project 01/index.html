About Event

Be at the forefront of global FSI leaders'
community in 2022

Since its inception, World Financial Innovation Series (WFIS) has grown from a single annual event in Africa to the most sought-after name in FSI globally. Today, WFIS is held annually in Africa and different regions in the Middle East & Southeast Asia for the fintech providers and financial institutions to find a common ground.

The events under WFIS are driven by the needs of the FSI community, striving to bring the best of innovation and technology in the industry. WFIS inspires professionals at all career levels, encouraging growth and collaboration among financial institutions, best-in-class fintech experts, researchers, and leaders in the public and private sectors.

WFIS Exhibition is the epicenter of where business happens at the events, featuring hundreds of top solution providers and innovators showcasing the latest tools, technologies and services supporting the global FSI community.
